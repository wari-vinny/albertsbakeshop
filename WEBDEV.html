Our Cake Collection

Albert’s Bakeshop didn’t start as a big plan—it actually began in a tiny home kitchen with a secondhand oven and a lot of late-night baking. Back then, Albert was just working a regular office job, making cakes on weekends for friends and family. He liked making each one personal, whether it was based on a funny memory, a favorite hobby, or a special moment. People started noticing, sharing photos online, and before he knew it, orders were coming in faster than he expected. Instead of rushing to grow too quickly, Albert kept things simple—taking his time with each design, talking to customers, and making sure every cake tasted just as good as it looked. Over time, that small hobby turned into a cozy little business that people love, not just for the cakes, but for the stories behind them.

We are located at Sgt. P Bernardo, Brgy. Caniogan, Pasig, Philippines.
